## 4. How It Works Section
**Title:** How the Project Model Works
**Subtitle:** A transparent workflow from initial funding to final profit distribution.

**Steps:**
1. **Capital Contribution:** Volunteers contribute capital (Min ₹1,00,000) to fund specific upcoming web series or movie projects.
2. **Content Production:** Capital is deployed exclusively for writing, casting, shooting, and post-production of the selected project.
3. **Release & Revenue:** Project generates revenue through Subscriptions, Ads, Licensing, and Brand Integrations.
4. **Profit Sharing:** 20% of net profit is distributed to the Network and Rank Achievers. 80% is retained for company growth.

## 7. Risk Disclosure Section
**Title:** Important Risk Disclosure
**Intro:** Before participating in this Network Incentive Model, you must read, understand, and accept the high-risk nature of content production financing.

**Risk Items:**
1. **High Risk Industry:** Content production is high-risk. Market trends shift rapidly, and past success does not dictate future results.
2. **Returns Not Guaranteed:** Revenue depends entirely on market performance (views, ads, rights sold). Fixed Bonus is strictly not guaranteed.
3. **Actual Realized Profits:** Profit distribution occurs ONLY from actual realized NET profit of a specific project. Loss-making projects distribute 0%.
4. **Regulatory Compliance:** We operate transparently. Consult your legal/financial advisor regarding your Participation Scheme.
